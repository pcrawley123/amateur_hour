About Me and My Blog

I'm the type of person who likes to do a lot of different things. A lot a lot. In my relatively brief life, I have forayed into:

  • Fiction Writing
  • Journalism
  • Computer Programming
  • Book Blogging
  • Considering Becoming a Social Media Personality
  • Knitting
  • Sewing
  • Watercolour Painting
  • Fashion Design
  • Photography
  • DIY Science
  • Various Styles of Dance
  • Theatre and Drama
  • Foreign Languages
  • And Much, Much More
The unfortunate problem that inevitably accompanies so many varied interests is a lack of any real commitment or mastery in any of them. As a result, I know several conversational phrases in Welsh, I can draw a gray scale in seconds flat, I can build a basic HTML page (like this one!), and I can tap like no one's watching. But if you were to ask me to deliver a Welsh speech, or create a professional-looking webpage, I would be utterly lost.

But that hasn't stopped me yet. I've always wanted to share what I do with the world, but the knowledge that I have absolutely zero authority eventually shuts me down. Then I realizes something: there are a lot of people who know a lot less than me. And, if I have any luck at all, those people might find they prefer someone a little less informed.
